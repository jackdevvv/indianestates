FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your service charges?

Our property buying assistance is FREE for customers. For property selling, we charge a nominal brokerage of 1-2% on the transaction value. Home loan processing is also free as we receive commission from partner banks.

How quickly can you help me find a property?

Based on your requirements, we can shortlist suitable properties within 24-48 hours. Site visits can be arranged immediately. On average, our customers find their dream home within 2-4 weeks.

Do you provide home loan assistance?

Yes, we have tie-ups with all major banks including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, and Axis Bank. We help you get pre-approved loans at competitive interest rates with minimum documentation. The service is completely free.

Are all your listed properties verified?

Yes, every property listed with us goes through a thorough verification process including title check, encumbrance verification, RERA compliance, and physical inspection. We only list legally clear properties.

Can NRIs buy property through you?

Absolutely! We have a dedicated NRI desk that handles all aspects of property purchase for overseas Indians, including virtual property tours, power of attorney arrangements, and NRI home loans.

What documents do I need to buy a property?

Basic documents include ID proof (Aadhaar/PAN), address proof, income proof (salary slips/ITR), and bank statements. For home loans, additional documents may be required. Our team will guide you through the complete list.
